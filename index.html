Our Story

Built by practitioners,
not theorists

Apex Studio was founded in 2017 by a small group of designers and strategists who believed the best agencies were too slow and the fastest studios were too careless. We set out to prove a different model was possible.

Today we work with a curated roster of clients — from ambitious startups to established enterprises — who value craft as much as we do. Our team of 14 spans strategy, visual identity, interface design, and growth.

We take on fewer projects than we're offered, so we can give each one our full attention. That's not a positioning statement. It's how we sleep at night.

FAQ

Questions, answered

How does the purchase process work? +

Select your service tier on the Services page and complete checkout via Stripe. You'll receive a confirmation email with a brief onboarding questionnaire and instructions for scheduling your kickoff call — typically within 48 hours of purchase.

Can I upgrade my package after purchase? +

Yes. If you find mid-project that you need more scope, we'll simply credit what you've paid toward the higher tier. Downgrades are handled case-by-case depending on work already completed — reach out and we'll find a fair resolution.

What's your refund policy? +

If we haven't started work yet, we offer a full refund within 7 days of purchase, no questions asked. Once the project is underway, refunds are prorated based on work completed. We've never had a client request one — we'd rather fix any issues than part ways.

Do you work with international clients? +

Absolutely. Roughly 40% of our clients are outside the US. We work asynchronously across time zones and schedule calls to overlap with your business hours. Stripe handles multi-currency billing seamlessly.

How long does a typical project take? +

Essentials engagements are typically 2 weeks. Studio projects run 4–6 weeks depending on feedback cycles. Enterprise partnerships are ongoing by design. All timelines are confirmed at kickoff based on your specific scope.

Can I speak to someone before purchasing? +

Of course. Use the contact form and we'll schedule a 20-minute discovery call — no sales pressure, just a conversation to see if we're a good fit. We turn down projects that aren't right for us, and we respect when clients do the same.
