FAQ

Questions, answered

How do I book a delivery? +

Fill out the contact form or call us directly. Give us the pickup and drop-off location, a description of your freight, and your preferred date. We'll confirm availability and send you a quote within one business day.

What kind of freight can you handle? +

We handle a wide range of freight including palletized goods, furniture, appliances, equipment, and general cargo. Our 26 ft box truck fits loads up to about 10,000 lbs and 1,500 cubic ft. Not sure if your load qualifies? Just ask — we'll let you know.

Do you have a lift gate? +

Yes. Our truck is equipped with a lift gate and a pallet jack, so we can load and unload at locations without a loading dock — including driveways, parking lots, and ground-level storefronts. No extra fee for lift gate use.

How far do you travel? +

We cover local deliveries within 50 miles and regional hauls up to 300+ miles. For longer distances or dedicated freight contracts, reach out and we'll discuss routing and pricing specific to your needs.

How much does a delivery cost? +

Pricing starts at $250 for local hauls and $600 for regional freight. Contract and recurring delivery rates are custom-quoted based on volume and route. All pricing is transparent — no hidden fuel surcharges or surprise fees.

Can I talk to someone before booking? +

Absolutely. Use the contact form and we'll get back to you within one business day. For urgent freight needs, calling is the fastest way to reach us. We'd rather answer your questions upfront than have any surprises on pickup day.
